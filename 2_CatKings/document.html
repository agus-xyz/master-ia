Roma and Xena

Anthropomorphic portraits of my 2 cats depicted as a noble kings from the Renaissance era.


Intro

As the assignment for Module 3, I went into creating a high-quality, regal portrait of my 2 house pet kitty sister cats, Xena and Roma. They should portrayed as what they really are: noble kings from the Renaissance era.

The artwork needed to depict the cat in an anthropomorphic manner, incorporating elements of historical grandeur while maintaining the likeness of the cat.


First main thoughts and ideas:


  • Powerful and high-quality: The generated images should be clean, powerful, and high-quality.
  • Frugality: The project should be achieved using available licenses (ChatGPT Pro) or other free use solutions.
  • Time efficiency: The project should be carried out under a couple of hours max. If longer time would be needed, a new simpler goal should be redefined.
  • One at a time: For efficiency and better results, we would generate the 2 wanted portraits sequentially, one at a time.
